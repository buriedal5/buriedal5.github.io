Mastermind Board Game
=====================
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mastermind_(board_game)#/media/File:Mastermind.jpg

Sample Game
-----------
Available Colors:
[G]reen
[B]lue
[O]range
[P]urple
[R]ed
[Y]ellow

Orange peg = Exact = Matches position and color
White peg = Partial = Matches color only

Scoring: (Exact, Partial)

Secret:  G B O P
Guess 1: Y Y B B (0, 1)
Guess 2: P O O Y (1, 1)
Guess 3: G P G Y (1, 1)
Guess 4: R R P Y (0, 1)
Guess 5: B O G P (1, 3)
Guess 6: G B O P (4, 0)
